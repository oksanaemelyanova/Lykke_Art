Whimsy, Wardrobe & Wonder

Each doll wears a story - ispired by outfits, stitched with soul

Lina

Soft steps, bold heart

Wrapped in warmth and attitude, Lina struts between sweetness and edge. Her knitted golfs hug her legs like a love letter to winter, while golden UGGs shimmer with unexpected charm. Netted tights and a short dress bring just the right touch of rebel — she's the girl who owns the room without saying a word.

Tova

Wander soft, dream loud

Tova is the girl who turns sidewalks into stories. Her wide beige top billows with ease, paired with a flirty short skirt and comfy knitted golfs for that perfect blend of cozy and carefree. With golden UGGs on her feet and her trusty Eastpak backpack slung over one shoulder, she's always ready for her next adventure. A tiny fish toy dangles playfully — a quiet reminder she never outgrew her daydreams. High ponytail? Always. She’s grounded, stylish, and secretly magical.

Raisa

Soft armor, steady soul

Raisa doesn’t rush. She stands. Wrapped in earth-toned layers — a wool skirt, cozy sweater, and chunky boots with hand-knit socks climbing high — she holds warmth like a shield. Her pompom hat adds a gentle flair, but don’t be fooled: behind that softness is a girl who knows exactly where she stands and why. She’s not loud, but the ground listens when she walks.
